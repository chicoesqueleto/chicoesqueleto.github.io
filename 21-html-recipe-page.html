Chickpea Hummus Recipe

A nutritive, delicious and flexible spreadable that takes your meals and snacks to the next level. Hummus is very easy to make, and you will love it.

Ingredients

  • half a pound of chickpeas.
  • 200 gr of sesame seeds.
  • A lemon.
  • 2 garlic cloves.
  • A pinch of paprika.
  • Salt to taste.

Instructions

  1. Soak the chickpeas in water for at least 8 hours.
  2. Remove the water from the chickpeas, add them to a pressure cooker, and boil for about 40 mins.
  3. Dry the chickpeas and keep the water used for boilng
  4. Tost the sesame seads for about 5 minutes, let them cool, and pass them to a food processor adding salt and process. Slowly add water from the chickpeas untill you get a creamy texture.
  5. Open the garlic cloves, remove the inside vein, press with the flat part of the knife and chop finely or use a macerator.
  6. Add the chickpeas, sesame seeds, garlic, lemon juice and salt to the processor, slowly add water from the chickpeas untill you get a creamy texture.
