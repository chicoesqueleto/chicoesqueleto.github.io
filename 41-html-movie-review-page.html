Roman Holiday

"Roman Holiday" is a 1953 romantic comedy about a princess named Ann, who escapes her royal duties while on a tour of Europe. In Rome, she meets an American reporter, Joe, who initially doesn't recognize her, and they spend a day exploring the city, leading to unexpected romance and adventure.
